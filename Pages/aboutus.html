About Us - Scholris

Welcome to Scholris, your trusted platform dedicated to connecting students with valuable scholarship opportunities. Founded by Biplaw Tiwari, Bimochan Poudel, and Trisha Shrestha, Scholris aims to empower students by providing easy access to scholarship information. Our mission is to eliminate barriers to education and support students in achieving their academic ambitions.

At Scholris, we understand the importance of financial assistance in pursuing higher education. Whether you're a high school senior, a college student, or a graduate student, our platform offers a comprehensive database of scholarships tailored to your needs. We strive to make the scholarship search process straightforward and effective, ensuring that every student can find opportunities that match their aspirations.

Join us at Scholris and take the first step towards a brighter academic future. Explore our resources, connect with a supportive community, and discover the scholarships that can help you reach your educational goals.
